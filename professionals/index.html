Robert S. Reder

Managing Partner



Robert is the managing partner of the firm. He was raised in New York City and became a permanent resident of Arizona in 2005. Robert obtained his Juris Doctorate at Suffolk University Law School in Boston, Massachusetts and Master of Laws at University College London in London, England. Robert practiced law with the Arizona offices of two of the largest law firms in the country before forming Blythe Grace. Robert represents individuals, small businesses and large companies located in Arizona and nationwide.

Robert has represented clients in cases ranging from small breach of contract actions to complex commercial disputes. Robert frequently appears in Arizona's courts and regularly conducts arbitrations, mediations, and administrative hearings.

Robert assists businesses with a variety of employment matters including human resource management, creating and implementing policies, and defense of discrimination and ADA lawsuits, EEOC charges, and investigations by the Arizona Attorney General's Office and the U.S. Department of Labor. He also works with entrepreneurs to start and grow their businesses.

Robert is involved in numerous community activities including working with the Volunteer Lawyer's Program on adoptions and guardianships and he has earned numerous awards for his work.

Robert lives an active lifestyle and regularly enjoys hiking, running, CrossFit, swimming, biking, snowboarding, and traveling. He also competes in triathlons and marathons.

Julie LaFave

Partner





Julie A. LaFave was born in California. As the daughter of a career Air Force Officer, she was raised on Air Force bases all over the world- although she counts Colorado Springs, Colorado as her hometown. She graduated magna cum laude from the University of Kentucky with a degree in social work, then completed a second degree in Psychology from Arizona State University. Julie received her Juris Doctorate from Arizona State University College of Law in 2000.

Julie practiced law at some of the biggest firms in Arizona for nearly 15 years. Julie joined Blythe Grace PLLC as a partner in 2015. Her practice focuses on business disputes, civil and commercial litigation, arbitration and mediation, and general corporate matters. Julie also brings to Blythe Grace substantial trial experience in both state and federal courts.

Julie's spare time is devoted to her family, including her husband Andy, a high school history teacher, and her sons Ryan and Caden. The LaFaves enjoy exploring nature and anything that keeps them outside and active. Julie also loves to travel and read.

Patti A. Jennings

Paralegal & Firm Administrator

Patti was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. Her family moved from Massachusetts to Arizona in 1948. Patti graduated from Shadow Mountain High School and received her Associate Degree in Paralegal Studies from Phoenix College in 1985.

Patti worked for some of the largest firms in Phoenix before becoming a Paralegal and Firm Administrator for Blythe Grace. Patti brings to the firm over thirty years of experience working with matters involving litigation, employment law, real estate, and corporate transactions. She has also assisted with numerous trials.

Patti enjoys giving back to the community and regularly assists the firm with its philanthropic work. Patti is a notary public.

Patti is active in the pet rescue community, fostering and volunteering for Arizona Small Dog Rescue. She enjoys taking care of her six dogs, and spending time working out, camping, gardening and reading.
