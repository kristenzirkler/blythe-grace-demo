Our Firm

Your problems will be addressed with the care you would expect from family.

Named for the managing attorney’s sisters – Emma Blythe Reder and Nora Grace Reder - Blythe Grace was formed to get outside of the cookie-cutter approach used by big firms.

Litigation is costly in time, money, and emotional strain. Partner with a team that can help you avoid litigation when possible. When litigation is unavoidable, expect aggressive representation in court.

Blythe Grace is committed to supporting and improving all of Arizona's communities.

Our Values

Dedication.

We are dedicated to our work and providing our clients with a superior product at an affordable cost.

Responsiveness.

We return every call, letter, and email as quickly as possible. Clients work with us, not our voicemail.

Partnership.

We partner with our clients. As a small business, we understand the challenges you face.

Straightforward.

We cut through the legal jargon and get to the point, without wasting valuable time.

Professionalism.

As seasoned professionals, we know our strengths and our limitations. If a matter is outside of our specialty, we partner to get the job done.

Integrity.

Acting ethically and responsibly, we use all of the tools available to lawyers without compromising our values or our client’s interests.

Passion.

We are passionate about our work. We are relentless in representing our clients and helping them achieve their goals.

Family.

We are more than a group of legal professionals – we are a family. We surround ourselves with positive, innovative, diverse and respectful people.
